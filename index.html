Pokétwo Commands
Discover the full list of Pokétwo commands with this guide. Easily navigate through the Pokétwo Bot commands list and find the perfect one for your needs.
View Pokétwo Discord Bot

Here are the Commands for Pokétwo Bot:

@Pokétwo unmeqa
Switch a pokémon back to its non-mega form.
@Pokétwo location
View or set the location for this server.
@Pokétwo redeem
Use a redeem to receive a pokémon of your choice.
@Pokétwo catch
Catch a wild Pokémon when it appears.
@Pokétwo pokémon
View your Pokémon collection.
@Pokétwo battle
Battle another trainer's Pokémon.
@Pokétwo trade
Trade Pokémon with another user.
@Pokétwo select
Select a Pokémon from your collection.
