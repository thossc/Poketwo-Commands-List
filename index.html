Pokétwo Commands
Discover the full list of Pokétwo commands with this guide. Easily navigate through the Pokétwo Bot commands list and find the perfect one for your needs.
Starting
@Pokétwo start
Start your Pokémon journey and receive your first Pokémon.
@Pokétwo tutorial
Learn the basics of how to play Pokétwo.
@Pokétwo help
View the help menu with all available commands.
@Pokétwo prefix
Check or change the command prefix for this server.
@Pokétwo ping
Check if the bot is online and responsive.
Catching and Collections
@Pokétwo catch [pokémon-name]
Catch a wild Pokémon when it appears.
@Pokétwo pokémon
View your Pokémon collection.
@Pokétwo select [number]
Select a Pokémon from your collection.
@Pokétwo release [number]
Release a Pokémon from your collection.
@Pokétwo info [number]
View detailed information about a Pokémon.
Market and Trading
@Pokétwo trade @user
Trade Pokémon with another user.
@Pokétwo market search [pokémon]
Search for Pokémon on the market.
@Pokétwo market buy [id]
Buy a Pokémon from the market.
@Pokétwo market sell [number] [price]
Sell a Pokémon on the market.
@Pokétwo auction start [number] [price]
Start an auction for a Pokémon.
Battling
@Pokétwo battle @user
Challenge another user to a battle.
@Pokétwo duel @user
Duel another user with selected Pokémon.
@Pokétwo moves [number]
View the moves of your selected Pokémon.
@Pokétwo use [move]
Use a move in battle.
@Pokétwo forfeit
Forfeit the current battle.
Shop and Items
@Pokétwo shop
View the item shop.
@Pokétwo buy [item]
Buy an item from the shop.
@Pokétwo inventory
View your inventory items.
@Pokétwo useitem [item] [number]
Use an item on a Pokémon.
@Pokétwo redeem [pokémon]
Use a redeem to receive a Pokémon of your choice.
Leveling and Evolution
@Pokétwo level [number]
Check the level of your selected Pokémon.
@Pokétwo evolve
Evolve your selected Pokémon if possible.
@Pokétwo exp
Check the experience points of your selected Pokémon.
@Pokétwo train
Train your Pokémon to gain experience.
@Pokétwo mega
Mega evolve your Pokémon if possible.
Some more stuffs
@Pokétwo daily
Claim your daily reward.
@Pokétwo weekly
Claim your weekly reward.
@Pokétwo leaderboard
View the server leaderboard.
@Pokétwo profile
View your profile and stats.
@Pokétwo achievements
View your achievements.
Spawning
@Pokétwo spawn
Force spawn a Pokémon (admin only).
@Pokétwo spawnrate
Check or set the spawn rate for this server.
@Pokétwo spawnchannel [channel]
Set the channel where Pokémon will spawn.
@Pokétwo shinyhunt [pokémon]
Start shiny hunting for a specific Pokémon.
@Pokétwo chain
Check your current shiny hunting chain.
Did you know?

Pokémon spawn every 20-25 messages (2 users min) in any channels

The spawn rate may change during events

Shiny chance: (~0.0244%), determined after catching, not during spawn, but this chance increases significantly for specific Pokémon through Shiny Hunting (chaining), though the odds improve less dramatically at higher chain counts.

Use `@Pokétwo catch <pokémon-name>` when one appears

Normal spawns: Regular Pokémon

Legendary spawns: Very rare (mostly low chance and obtainable)

Event spawns: During special events
