View Pokétwo Discord Bot
Did you know?

Pokémon spawn every 20-25 messages (2 users min) in any channels

The spawn rate may change during events

Shiny chance: (~0.0244%), determined after catching, not during spawn, but this chance increases significantly for specific Pokémon through Shiny Hunting (chaining), though the odds improve less dramatically at higher chain counts.

Use `@Pokétwo catch <pokémon-name>` when one appears

Normal spawns: Regular Pokémon

Legendary spawns: Very rare (mostly low chance and obtainable)

Event spawns: During special events

Here are the Commands for Pokétwo Bot:
@Pokétwo unmega
Switch a pokémon back to its non-mega form.
@Pokétwo location
View or set the location for this server.
@Pokétwo redeem
Use a redeem to receive a pokémon of your choice.
@Pokétwo favoritall
Mass favorite selected pokemon.
@Pokétwo donate
Donate to receive shards.
