Pokétwo Commands
Discover the full list of Pokétwo commands with this guide. Easily navigate through the Pokétwo Bot commands list and find the perfect one for your needs.
Starting
@Pokétwo start
View the starter pokémon.
@Pokétwo pick <pokemon>
Pick a starter pokémon to get started.
@Pokétwo help
Show help about the bot, a command, or a category.
@Pokétwo ping
View the bot's latency.
@Pokétwo invite
View the invite link for the bot.
@Pokétwo stats
View bot info.
Catching and Collections
@Pokétwo catch <pokémon-name>
Catch a wild pokémon.
@Pokétwo pokemon
View or filter the pokémon in your collection.
@Pokétwo select <number>
Select a specific pokémon from your collection.
@Pokétwo release <number>
Release pokémon from your collection for 2pc each.
@Pokétwo info <number>
View a specific pokémon from your collection.
@Pokétwo favorite <number>
Mark a pokémon as a favorite.
@Pokétwo unfavorite <number>
Unfavorite a selected pokemon.
@Pokétwo favoriteall
Mass favorite selected pokemon.
@Pokétwo unfavoriteall
Mass unfavorite selected pokemon.
@Pokétwo nickname <name>
Change the nickname for your pokémon.
@Pokétwo nickall <name>
Mass nickname pokémon from your collection.
@Pokétwo releaseall
Mass release pokémon from your collection for 2 pc each.
@Pokétwo reindex
Re-number all pokémon in your collection.
@Pokétwo order
Change how your pokémon are ordered.
@Pokétwo pokedex
View your pokédex, or search for a pokémon species.

@Pokétwo trade addall

@Pokétwo trade add pokecoins <amount>
Add Pokécoin(s) to a trade.
@Pokétwo trade add redeems <amount>

@Pokétwo trade remove <number>

@Pokétwo trade remove redeems <amount>

@Pokétwo trade info
View a pokémon from the trade.
@Pokétwo trade confirm

@Pokétwo trade cancel
Cancel a trade.

Buy or sell pokémon on the Pokétwo marketplace.
@Pokétwo market search <pokémon>

@Pokétwo market buy <id>

@Pokétwo market add <number> <price>

@Pokétwo market remove <id>

@Pokétwo market info <id>
View a pokémon from the market.
@Pokétwo auction

@Pokétwo auction start <number> <price>

@Pokétwo auction bid <id> <amount>
Bid on an auction.
@Pokétwo auction info <id>

Spawning

Force spawn a Pokémon (admin only).

Redirect pokémon catches to one or more channels.
@Pokétwo redirect reset
Reset channel redirect.
@Pokétwo stopincense
Stop an incense.
Did you know?

Pokémon spawn every 20-25 messages (2 users min) in any channels

The spawn rate may change during events

Shiny chance: (~0.0244%), determined after catching, not during spawn, but this chance increases significantly for specific Pokémon through Shiny Hunting (chaining), though the odds improve less dramatically at higher chain counts.

Use @Pokétwo catch <pokémon-name> when one appears

Normal spawns: Regular Pokémon

Legendary spawns: Very rare (mostly low chance and obtainable)

Event spawns: During special events

Exact Odds

Exact odds for gigantamax pokemon is 1/100 and the odds to catch one in the wild is 1/9695, making the odds of a shiny 1/33089719
