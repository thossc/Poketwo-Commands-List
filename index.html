Pokétwo Commands

Discover the full list of Pokétwo commands with this guide. Easily navigate through the Pokétwo Bot commands list and find the perfect one for your needs.

All commands start with @Pokétwo or p! prefix. You can also mention the bot directly.

Here are the Commands for Pokétwo Bot:

@Pokétwo unmega [pokémon]
Switch a pokémon back to its non-mega form.
@Pokétwo location [region/city]
View or set the location for this server.
@Pokétwo redeem [pokémon]
Use a redeem to receive a pokémon of your choice.
@Pokétwo catch [pokémon_name]
Attempt to catch a wild Pokémon when it appears.
@Pokétwo pokémon
View your Pokémon collection with levels, IVs, and stats.
@Pokétwo battle @user
Challenge another trainer to a Pokémon battle.
@Pokétwo trade @user
Trade Pokémon with another user in the server.
@Pokétwo info [pokémon]
Get detailed information about a specific Pokémon.
@Pokétwo select [pokémon_number]
Select a Pokémon from your collection to use.
@Pokétwo pokedex [pokémon]
View Pokédex entry for a specific Pokémon.
