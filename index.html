How to use Pokétwo commands

All commands start with the prefix @Pokétwo or p!. You can also mention the bot instead of using the prefix. Below you'll find a comprehensive list of commands with examples and descriptions.

unmeqa

Pokémon Transformation

@Pokétwo unmeqa [pokémon]
Switch a Pokémon back to its non-mega form. This command is useful if you want to revert a Mega evolved Pokémon back to its original form.
Pokémon Management

location

Server Location

@Pokétwo location [region/city]
View or set the location for this server. Setting a location affects which Pokémon spawn in your server. Different regions have different Pokémon available.
Server Settings

redeem

Reward Redemption

@Pokétwo redeem [pokémon]
Use a redeem to receive a Pokémon of your choice. Redeems are special items that allow you to obtain any Pokémon, including legendaries and shinies.
Rewards & Items

catch

Catch Pokémon

@Pokétwo catch [pokémon_name]
Attempt to catch a wild Pokémon when it appears. You need to be quick as Pokémon will flee after a short time. Use the correct spelling of the Pokémon's name.
Catching

pokémon

View Collection

@Pokétwo pokémon
View your Pokémon collection. This command shows all the Pokémon you've caught, their levels, IVs, and other stats. You can also filter and sort your collection.
Collection

battle

Battle Other Trainers

@Pokétwo battle @user
Challenge another trainer to a Pokémon battle. Battles are turn-based and use the stats of your Pokémon. Winning battles earns you experience and rewards.
Battling
